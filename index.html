Math IV - Quarter 4

Week 1: Favorite Fruits Survey

A school conducted a survey among 50 students to determine their favorite fruits. The results were:
1. Apple: 15 students
2. Banana: 12 students
3. Mango: 8 students
4. Orange: 5 students
5. Other: 10 students

1. Which fruit is the most popular?

2. How many students prefer other fruits?

3. Which fruits are preferred by fewer than 10 students?

4. What percentage of students prefer apples?

5. Which inference can be made from the survey?

Week 2: Student Subject Preferences

A survey of 100 high school students revealed their favorite subjects:
1. Mathematics: 30 students
2. Science: 25 students
3. English: 20 students
4. History: 15 students
5. Other: 10 students

1. Which subject is the most popular?

2. How many students prefer History?

3. Which subjects are preferred by more than 20 students?

4. What percentage of students prefer English?

5. What can be inferred from the survey?

Week 3: Water Consumption Patterns

A utility company tracked monthly water consumption (in gallons) for 50 households:
1. January: 5,000
2. February: 4,500
3. March: 6,000
4. April: 7,000
5. May: 8,000
6. June: 9,000

1. What is the overall trend in water consumption?

2. Which month showed the highest water consumption?

3. What percentage increase occurred from January to June?

4. Which inference can be made about summer months?

5. What action might the utility company consider?
